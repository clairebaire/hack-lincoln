October 24 - 26, 2014
overview

Hack Lincoln is a 48-hour hackathon bringing together the area's hackers - developers, technologists, designers, and civic advocates - to wrangle data sets, learn new ideas and techniques, and build some cool stuff in a fun environment.

Open Nebraska is a citizen-led organization dedicated to promoting civic engagement and furthering the open data movement in Nebraska. It's mission is to equip citizens with tools to create positive change and solve community problems through civic application development, open data advocacy, and tech education.

We'll supply the food, guidance, and real-world problems and you bring your laptop, appetite, skills and ideas.
